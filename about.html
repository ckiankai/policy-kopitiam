About Policy Kopitiam

Buying insurance can feel like ordering kopi with a menu full of unfamiliar terms. This site is a neutral place to learn the mechanics first.

Why this site exists

I built this after trying to learn insurance myself, and realising many people end up deciding before they even understand the vocabulary and trade-offs.

Along the way, I wanted to bridge theory and practice, taking what I learn in school and internships and translating it into the situations people actually face when dealing with insurance in real life.

There isn’t really an “all-in-one” place that explains how the pieces fit together, so this is my attempt to build something that helps others ask better questions before committing to long-term decisions.

The goal is clarity without selling: principles, mechanics, and simple frameworks so people can ask better questions.

What you will not find here

  • No personalised advice
  • No insurer comparisons, rankings, or premium tables
  • No “best plan” lists
  • No sales or lead-generation content
